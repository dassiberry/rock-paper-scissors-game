Rock, Paper, Scissors Game

How to play:

  1. Choose Rock, Paper, or Scissors by clicking an image or pressing R, P, or S.
  2. The computer will randomly select an option.
  3. The winner is determined based on whoever wins:
    • Rock beats Scissors.
    • Scissors beats Paper.
    • Paper beats Rock.
Make your move!
